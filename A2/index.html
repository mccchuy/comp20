Dedicated to first generation success

At Malden to College (M2C), our mission as a non-profit is to provide first-generation students with the tools and knowledge to prepare themselves and their families for their journey and completion of a 4-year undergraduate program at a college or university.
Since 2009, we have been providing year-round college readiness services to students from 9th grade through college graduation.
